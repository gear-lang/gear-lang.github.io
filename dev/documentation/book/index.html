Introduction

The Gear Programming Language

Welcome. By opening...err... navigating your browser to this book, you've taken your first step on an exciting journey. If you hadn't guessed by the title, this book will teach you about the Gear Programming Language. It will also (hopefully) entertain you along the way.

Your probably wondering why the world needs another programming langauge, after all, there are so many already! But languages, like tools, are each made for a different purpose. Gear was primarly designed as an embeddable scripting langaugage. That means you can expose your applications functionality to your uses allowing them extend your app for good or evil purposes.

The contents of this book focus on the Gear language itself. If you want to know more about embedding Gear, then checkout the embedders guide.

Hello World

No programming language book is complete without the famous Hello World program.
